about

Visual media exposes us to false realities and unrealistic expectations, often sparking a shift
in perspective of how we view ourselves. A jarring example is the significant influence social
media and technology have on teenagers and young adults, shaping the way they perceive
their self and body image. With the growth of technology and constant exposure to influencers
and digital media, and specifically, filters, it can be said that there has been a drastic increase
in pressure on how we should ideally look like.
